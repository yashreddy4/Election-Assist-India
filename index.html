Lok Sabha Elections

Members of Parliament (MPs) are elected directly by the people to represent their constituencies in the lower house of India's bicameral Parliament.

State Assembly Elections

Members of Legislative Assembly (MLAs) are elected directly by the people to form the state government.

The Election Commission

The Election Commission of India (ECI) is an autonomous constitutional authority responsible for administering election processes in India.
